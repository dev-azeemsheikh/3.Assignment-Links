About Us


The Founder/Patron

To keep up with modern times and the latest marketing and allied techniques the Car o’clock was founded in the year 1966 at 104 Hotel Metropole Karachi, by Mr. Mohammad Hussain Dhanji, a perfect soft spoken, humble, modest person standing as a pioneer in Automotive Industry. An eminent well known national personality and leader of trading community.

He made an energetic and painstaking efforts to upgrade the Car o’clock in to a grand showroom initially at I.I Chundigar Road, then P.E.C.H.S now in the posh area on main Clifton road and D.H.A Islamabad. His selfless, relentless pursuit and devotion of finance and untiring efforts towards community upliftment and social welfare for betterment of trade association are legendary. As the third generation of Dhanji family joined the group, the business expanded immensely and the turnover of Car o’clock increased many folds.
